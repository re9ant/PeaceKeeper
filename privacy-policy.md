# Privacy Policy — PeaceKeeper

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview
PeaceKeeper ("the app") is a call-screening app developed by re9ant.
This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection
PeaceKeeper does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Contacts Permission
The app requests access to your device contacts (`READ_CONTACTS`) solely to:
- Identify whether an incoming caller is in your address book
- Allow you to manually add specific contacts to a mute list

All contact data is processed **entirely on your device**. No contact
information is ever sent to any server, third party, or external service.

## Call Screening
Incoming call details are processed locally on your device only,
using Android's built-in Call Screening API. No call data leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services
The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

## Data Storage
Your mute list preferences are stored locally in your device's
app storage (SharedPreferences). This data is never backed up to
any external server.
